Implied odds and edge are key in identifying valuable bets.
Implied odds represent the probability of a parlay winning based on the given odds.
Edge factors in both probability and payout potential to determine the expected profitability.
Example: A bet with a 5% edge means that, on average, you can expect a $5 profit for every $100 wagered.
Payout boosts are offered daily, often one boost for each sport ranging from 20-100%, and are the most important feature of making pick6 profitable. Utilizing daily boost and the best picks from the lock machine will almost always have an expected profitability.
